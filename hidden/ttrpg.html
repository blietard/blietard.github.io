What is TTRPG ?

If you know what is a Tabletop Roleplay Game, you can skip this section.

A Tabletop Roleplay Game (TTRPG) is an asymetrical game in which basically one person (the Game Master) narrates a story. The other players (simply called Players) incarnate the main characters.
TTRPG is about sitting around a table and playing with imagination, roleplay, discussion, dice rolls and most importantly : fun (as many other genres of games, yes).
In a TTRPG, the Game Master not only narrates a story but they also invites the Players to take part in that story. Usually, the GM describes a delicate situation that the main characters have to face, and invites the Players to declare how their character should act in this situation.
Each Player has a character of their own with features distinct from other characters of the story. This can be outstanding physical abilities, brilliant or brainless mind, magical powers, etc. depending the type of story that is narrated. Players' characters are not the only ones of the story. The Game Master will roleplay Non-Playable Characters (NPCs) players can interact with.
Moreover, characters are not a 1-to-1 reflect of their players and the story isn't really happening. The Game Master will sometimes ask a player to roll dice to check if the character can perform a desired action.

BaBaGa is pleased to annouce the First Magnet TTRPG (MTTRPG) will be held in February (precise date to be determined) and is looking for extra players to join us.
The exact narrative setting of the game is not yet decided but will likely be medieval fantasy or cyberpunk.
Profiles of interest include, but are not limited to, the following :
  • people with experience in TTRPG ;
  • people with no experience with TTRPG ;
  • people having their own dice set ;
  • people not having a dice set ;
  • people interested in fantasy and/or cyberpunk ;
  • young people ;
  • people not falling into the previous category ;
  • any people in the team actually.
While the Game Mastering Committee has experience with TTRPG, it is not a mandatory requirement for Players. MTTRPG is open to new players willing to try TTRPG once in their lifetime.
Experience with any form of roleplay (drama/theater, other kinds of RPG, etc.) is neither required to enjoy TTRPG. This MTTRPG is a beginner-friendly event.
